Charms

Please see my Charms table for technical info.

Standard

These are:

  • Circular
    • : arcana, fortitude, power, agility, essence
  • Square
    • : wisdom, heart, soul, scavenger, bandit
  • Triangular
    • : nature, fire, mind, perception, ghost
  • Star
    • : blessing, soldier, winter, storm, monk

    Special

    These are: monk, ghost, essence and scavenger, and the hexagonal charms are exclusive. You should always keep these if you get them as they are very useful, except the latter.

    Hexagonal

    You will probably find ONE item (usually leg armor) with a hexagonal charm slot in it early in playing DH4 with any given character, and you will be able to add 3 other slots to it. Do not hold onto this waiting for hex charms – they are only available within the gold chest. You can’t even get them as a gift. Besides, even if you do get some from the chest, that leg armor will be around tier 15, with the best armor being tier 103.

    If you need other information about charms, you can find details like merge time, etc., at dh4.wikia.com/charms (please note that some info there is outdated.)
